Join Leighton Buzzrd RFC

At Leighton Buzzard Rugby Football Club, we aim to be a successful community club offering rugby, at all levels, for all. We always welcome new players and members to join us with or without any experience in playing rugby, whatever your age, skill or fitness. We have excellent rubgy facilities comprising four full size pitches including floodlit training areas, modern gym, recently upgraded changing rooms togetherwill fully qualified coaches and sports therapists. Our clubhouse and bar offer a vibrant social scenne which members and players can enjoy. Interested in plaing for Leighton Buzzard Rugby Club? PLease browse our website to find out about LBRFC - info on the club can be found on the Teams tab above. Alternatively, just turn up to a training session! We are a very friendly and welcoming club so please come along and enjoy your rugby! We look forward to you joining us!

Senior and Academy

Training sessions and home games are held at Wight's Meadow (LU7 9HR) for both Seniors and Academy Teams. Training for Mens Senior and Academy sides take place on Tuesday and Thursday evenings during this season, while Ladies train on a Tuesday evening.

The Minis

The Minis start from the uder 6 age group and run through to under 12s. During this time, we coach and teach boys & girls the skills and ethos of rugby, starting with tag rugby up to under 8s. After under 8s, we, then, slowly introduce the contact game inline with th RFU guidelines.

Mixed ability

Juniors start from the uder 13 age group for both boys & girls; the boys have age group teams at each age running through to under 16 (Year 11) age group. The Junior teams compete at festivals and competitions as wll as against both local teams and teams a little further afield.
